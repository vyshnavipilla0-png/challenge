What is Climate Change?

Climate change refers to significant changes in global temperatures and weather patterns over time. While some changes are natural, recent rapid shifts are mainly caused by human activities like burning fossil fuels and deforestation.

Why Does it Matter?

Rising temperatures lead to melting glaciers, sea-level rise, extreme weather, and ecosystem disruptions. These affect food security, water supply, human health, and biodiversity worldwide.
